About Sports Cubed

The Sportscubed project was developed as a result of a hack at the University of Edinburgh in 2013, a ‘Smart Data Hack’, held at the School of Informatics. The City of Edinburgh Council shared some data to support this event and took part in the judging. Sportscubed was one of the winners, a prototype developed by three first year students. The Council decided to develop the prototype and worked with the students to deliver an app that would encourage young people to take up more physical activity.
This app is an exciting and innovative digital product, bringing information about sports in the city to a wider audience. The project reflects the strategic approach to digital service delivery that the City of Edinburgh Council is taking, in line with its ICT and Digital Strategy.
