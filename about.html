About Sports Cubed and its authors

Yordan Hristov

Website and Design

Computers and technologies have been in my eyesight ever since I started High school since my profile there was Informatics and Mathematics. I have been doing web development for the last few years but areas like Artificial Intelligence and Low-level programming are also part of my interests. For the purpose of the project I was mainly using JavaScript, CSS3 and HTML5 from the technological point of view and Adobe Photoshop from a designer point of view. Apart from web development I also have experience in Python, Java, Haskell a bit in C and in SQL.
I am currently studying Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh. The degree is quite theoretical and therefore the Sports Cubed project with the City of Edinburgh Council was a great opportunity to gain some more practical experience. And I am not talking only about gaining technical knowledge but improving my networking and interpersonal skills as well. I learned how to effectively work in an agile environment as part of a team.
